RENTAL DRESS SYSTEM


                          TITLE:   FASHION ON RENT 


Summary:-

"FASHION ON RENT" is a multiple vendor supporting "Online Web Portal".This system involves three roles mainly Customer,Vendor and Admin.
Portal provides customers to rent Aparels online where customers can give their reviews based on their experience. Customers can easily
View, Select, Search, Cancel and Rent Aparels online. Vendors can put their Aparels for renting on this Portal. On this we are also providing
facility for customers to become Vendor by providing their registration details as Vendor. Admin being the host can manage the Product 
Catalogue and has right to approve the request made by Vendor to Upload and make changes in Aparel details.
