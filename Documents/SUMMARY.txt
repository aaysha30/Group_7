RENTAL DRESS SYSTEM


                          TITLE:   FASHION ON RENT 


Summary:-
It is a multiple vendor supporting "Online Web Portal".This system is managing three roles nainly Customer,Vendor and Admin.
Customers can book dresses on rent,can cancel booking,can search for dresses,can view dress.

we are providing customers to rent Aparels online and where customers can give their reviews based on their experience. Customers can easily View, 
Select, Search, Cancel and Rent Dresses online. On our System Vendors can put their Dresses for 
renting. On this we also providing  facility for customers to become Vendor  by providing their
registration details as Vendor. Admin being the host can manage the Product Catalogue and has right
to approve the request made by Vendor to Upload and make changes in Dress details.


Features:-
1)Offer vendors to expand their business.
2)A Customer can also become a small scale vendor by putting out their 1-2 dresses.
3)Filter products according to size,occasions.
4)Customer and Vendor can give their Complaints/feedback.
