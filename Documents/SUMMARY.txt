RENTAL DRESS SYSTEM


                          TITLE:   FASHION ON RENT 


Summary:-
"FASHION ON RENT" is a multiple vendor supporting "Online Web Portal".This system involves three roles mainly Customer,Vendor and Admin.
We are providing customers to rent Aparels online where customers can give their reviews based on their experience. Customers can easily
View, Select, Search, Cancel and Rent Dresses online. On our System Vendors can put their Dresses for renting. On this we also providing
facility for customers to become Vendor  by providing their registration details as Vendor. Admin being the host can manage the Product 
Catalogue and has right to approve the request made by Vendor to Upload and make changes in Dress details.


Features:-
1)Billing->Our system will provide auto billing system.
2)Avaibility->In this,Admin can check whether the dress from a particular category is available or not.
3)Search->Customer Can search by dress Categories.
4)CRM->Customer can give review,can put complaint and can provide feedback too.
5)View->Customer can view their order details,Vendor can also view their Inventory.
